Tiny Tanks
01/2022 - 05/2022
Java

In my second year of bachelor, We were tasked to make a game on java, with a team of 5 and in 6 months
It's a 2D shooter with a top view where the goal is to eliminate all the enemy tanks.
In this project I was in charge of :
- The tanks' movement
- The managment of collision of the tanks
- I've lent a hand with the missiles bounces
- Bonus management
- Some additional features, such as the furtivity in the bushes or the air raids

This project was interesting because it was at the time the longest project I have worked on.
It is also the project that made me want to pursue my studies in the field of game development
