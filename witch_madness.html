Witch Madness
01/2024 - 02/2024
Unreal Engine / C++

Game is playable HERE

It is currently my last game made by myself
It was made in one month

It's a "automatic runner" arcade game (like Subway Surfers or Temple Run)
The goal is to fight the witches coming your way and to power up in order to defeat the big bad boss Witch
This game has 2 playable characters with different abilities
I did everything by myself in this project, from programming to the assets or FXs

I was in charge of gameplay elements, such as :

  • - The semi-random enemy spawn
  • - The movement mechanic (auto move, side movements and teleportation)
  • - The different upgrades that the player can find in game
  • - The Sound Integration
