Tetrucks
02/2024 - 02/2024
Unity / C#

Game is playable HERE

It was a gamejam's project made with discord friends
It was a group project, we were 10 working on it for a month
The theme was "Space"

It is a puzzle arcade game.
The goal is to load the most truck possible before our boss gets angry and decide to fire us.
In order to do so, you must take packages coming on the conveyor belt and try to load them in the current truck.
The truck loading mechanic is very similar to a 3D Tetris.

I've helped for the implementation of gameplay elements such as :

  • - The package drag and drop mechanic
  • - The coming and going of the trucks
  • - And I did some polish, such as UI juiciness and the boss's emotions
