Little Red Riding Hood GO
09/2023 - 12/2023
Unreal Engine / C++

Game is playable HERE

It was my first and only project that I did while in "Mast√®re" at ICAN.
It was a group project, we were 7 working on it for 3 months

It was a Hitman-GO based puzzle game, mingled the Little Red Riding Hood IP.
The goal of the red riding hood is to reach granny's house while dodging the dangers awaiting for her in the forest
It is a turn by turn grid-based puzzle
The goal is to move smartly and wisely in order to reach the end case without trouble

I was in charge of gameplay elements, such as :

  • - Turn by turn actions
  • - A modular grid system, enabling the designers to link the cases they want with each other
  • - The ennemi "AI" using the A* for deplacement
  • - Pawn animations
