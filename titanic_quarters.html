Titanic Quarters
09/2024 - 09/2024
Unity / C#

Game is playable HERE

It was a project that I did with classmates for an ISART organized gamejam.
It was a group project, we were 8 to work on it for one week.
The theme was Fake News

It's a puzzle arcade game.
The goal is to crypt/decrypt the most letters before the panic jauge overflows
The game is split beetween 2 modes, one where we must send the letter to the corresponding desk.
And another where, depending of the letter we must translate the letter in one of the 3 following languages : Military,Nautic, Morse.

I was in charge of the implementation of some gameplay features, such as :

  • - The letter drag and drop into a desk minigame
  • - The morse and military minigames (with their languages)
  • - The sound integration (using FMOD)
  • - And I added some polish effects, such as the exagerated letter deplacement animation, or the basket fill based on the number of letters
