The app is dowloadable HERE

It is my personal project of creating a mobile app.

It is an app to create and play customizable quizzes
It was designed for helping to learn or study anything the user wants.
We are able to create and edit our quizzes however we see fit, varying between (2 at the moment) quiz types.
Of course, we can also play them whenever we want.

I was in charge of everything, including :

  • - The UI/UX design
  • - The possiblity to play 2 types of quizzes (Clickable and Typed)
  • - The possiblity to edit quizzes, right now we can edit :
    • - The title
    • - The description
    • - The type between the current ones (Clickable et Typed)
    • - The max time to complete
    • - Whether answers should be shuffled or not
    • - And at last, question and answers
  • - The capacity to save quizzes on your device

I will come back to it at one point, I want to add :

  • - The IOS port (I can't test right now, so I can't post a version without testing)
  • - Other quiz types
  • - QoL improvments such as being able to have multiple correct answer, for instance
